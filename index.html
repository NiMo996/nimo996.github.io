About Me

I’m Nickolas, a tech enthusiast currently pursuing an associate’s degree in Software Development with aspirations to transition from the automotive industry to a career in technology. I’m focused on improving my skills in programming, cybersecurity, and web development, with particular interests in Cybersecurity, Networks, and Object-oriented Programming. Balancing family, work, and studies is a priority for me, and I enjoy learning through hands-on projects, visual cues, and trial and error. I’m also working on certifications like CompTIA A+ and exploring ways to build an impressive tech portfolio.

Favorite Books

  • Search for Meaning by Viktor Frankl – A profound exploration of finding purpose even in the most difficult circumstances, which resonates with my journey of balancing life, work, and career transitions.
  • 1984 by George Orwell- A gripping look at surveillance and societal control, connecting to my interest in cybersecurity and ethical hacking.
  • The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho- A story of following dreams and finding one’s path, which inspires me to pursue my passion for technology.
  • Crime and Punishment by Fyodor Dostoevsky– A deep psychological exploration of morality, justice, and guilt, which speaks to the analytical side of my personality.
  • Pied Piper by Nevil Shute– A tale of survival and responsibility, reflecting some of the values I hold in both personal and professional life.
